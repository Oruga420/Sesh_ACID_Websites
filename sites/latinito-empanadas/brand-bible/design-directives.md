# Design Directives — Latinito Empanadas

## Identity Summary
Latinito Empanadas is a warm, family-run Latin American takeout spot in Brampton, Ontario, founded in 2020 by Matthew and his mother Graciela, who brought their Paraguayan empanada traditions to Canada. The brand is cheerful, bold, and proudly Latin-Canadian — yellow and red dominate, the logo features a maple leaf with "Est. 2020", and the overall feel is inviting, affordable, and fun. This is comfort food with cultural pride, not fine dining.

## DO
- Use a bright white background as the dominant page color — the site is clean and light
- Use bold yellow (#FFD700) and red (#E31B23) as the two primary brand colors everywhere
- Make headings BIG, BOLD, and UPPERCASE — their menu boards use massive display type
- Use real food photos from their Squarespace CDN in every card and section
- Include their actual menu items with real prices ($2.69/empanada, $0.75/dip, etc.)
- Show hours and address prominently — this is a takeout spot, people need to find it
- Feature their tagline "So Good, One Just Isn't Enough!" in the hero
- Mention Halal options (beef and chicken) — it's a key differentiator
- Include catering clients (SickKids, Toyota, Cargill, Dufferin Peel) as social proof
- Use rounded corners (8-12px) on cards — friendly and approachable
- Red borders on cards and sections — matches their combo menu board style

## DON'T
- Don't use dark backgrounds — this brand is bright, warm, and light
- Don't use Playfair Display or Inter — those are generic defaults, not this brand
- Don't use subtle or muted colors — Latinito is bold yellow and red, not pastels
- Don't use script or handwritten fonts — their typography is bold display sans-serif
- Don't fake stats (years experience, ratings bars) — they don't have these on their site
- Don't use stock photos — they have plenty of real product images
- Don't make it look "fine dining" — this is casual, affordable takeout
- Don't bury hours and address — location-first means these are above the fold priority

## Font Pairing
Heading: Passion One — bold, compressed display sans-serif that matches their menu board typography style (EMPANADAS, DIPS, CHURROS all in massive bold caps)
Body: Nunito Sans — warm, rounded sans-serif that matches the friendly, family-oriented tone; closest match to Squarespace's system sans-serif rendering

## Color Usage Rules
- Primary CTA: Red (#E31B23) button with white text on white backgrounds
- Section alternation: White (#FFFFFF) → Warm cream (#FFF8E1) → White
- Yellow (#FFD700) for decorative accents, logo background, badge/ribbon elements
- Red (#E31B23) for all headings, borders, underlines, and interactive elements
- Dark (#222222) for body text and footer background
- Never use blue or purple — completely off-brand
- Never use black backgrounds for content sections

## Layout Archetype: LOCATION-FIRST
Rationale: Latinito is a takeout empanada shop where foot traffic, hours, and neighborhood identity drive conversions. The menu is the primary content, followed by the brand story and location details.

## Section Order (from archetype)
1. Hero — atmosphere photo with tagline "So Good, One Just Isn't Enough!" + Order Now CTA
2. Menu/Featured Items — empanadas, dips, churros, combos with real photos and prices
3. About/Story — Matthew and Graciela's journey from Paraguay to Brampton
4. Gallery — food photos and catering showcase
5. Reviews — real customer quotes from Google/Wanderlog
6. Location + Hours — map, address, hours, phone
7. Contact — email, social links, order links

## Layout Blueprint
- Header: Sticky white header with yellow logo left, nav right (Menu, Story, Location, Contact), red "Order Now" CTA button
- Hero: Full-bleed image with dark overlay, centered white text, tagline + CTA
- Menu section: Grid of food cards with real product images, names, descriptions, prices
- Story section: Warm cream background, founder story with timeline imagery
- Reviews: White background, quote cards with red accent borders
- Location: Map embed + hours table + address + phone
- Footer: Dark (#222222) background, 3 columns (contact, hours, social links), yellow logo accent

## Tone of Voice Guide
- Headlines should sound like: "EMPANADAS" "CHURROS" "COMBOS" — single bold words or short punchy phrases
- Body copy should sound like: "Fresh dough made daily, filled to order. Eight delicious varieties to choose from."
- CTAs should sound like: "Order Now" "Come Try Something New!" "View Our Menu"
