# Design Directives — West Endz Hair Salon

## Identity Summary
West Endz Hair Salon is a 60+ year heritage salon in Alderwood, south Etobicoke. Run by Lucy Zollerano (master stylist, 25+ years) with experienced stylists Mary Taffuri and Darlene. The brand voice is "low-key, attitude-free" — warm, welcoming, and unpretentious. The visual identity centers on an elegant gold serif logo with green accents on a clean, light background. The salon serves a loyal, mature local clientele who value trust, experience, and community.

## DO
- Use warm, light backgrounds (#FAFAF7 and #F0EDE6) throughout — the site is light-themed
- Use the gold accent (#c4a35a) sparingly — for logo, premium accents, decorative elements
- Use green (#56c477) for interactive elements (buttons, links, CTAs)
- Use Cormorant Garamond for headings (elegant serif matching their refined logo)
- Use Lato for body text (warm, approachable sans-serif)
- Emphasize heritage: "60+ years", "Alderwood", "community"
- Show real testimonials with customer names
- Keep the tone warm and conversational — "your hair, your way"
- Use the dark salon tools image as a dramatic background section
- Include senior discount prominently (community-oriented perk)
- Feature each stylist individually with their philosophy
- Emphasize Mon Platin and Hair Treats product certifications

## DON'T
- Don't use dark mode — the brand is light and airy
- Don't use Playfair Display, Roboto, or Inter fonts
- Don't use Unsplash stock images — use their real website images
- Don't add fabricated stats bars
- Don't use corporate language ("solutions", "leverage")
- Don't make it feel high-end/luxury — it's approachable and neighborhood
- Don't use anchor navigation — every section needs its own page
- Don't use the template developer's social links (Qode Interactive)
- Don't forget the Mon Platin branding — it's a key differentiator

## Font Pairing
- **Heading**: Cormorant Garamond (400-700) — refined serif with heritage character
- **Body**: Lato (300-700) — clean, warm, readable sans-serif
- **Nav**: Lato 500, uppercase, tracking 0.12em
- **Rationale**: The pairing echoes the salon's dual personality — established elegance (60+ year heritage) meets modern approachability

## Color Usage Rules
- **Primary Green (#56c477)**: Buttons, CTAs, active states, links
- **Dark Green (#039146)**: Emphasis text, borders, dark accents
- **Gold (#c4a35a)**: Logo, decorative dividers, premium feel accents
- **Main Background (#FAFAF7)**: Page body — warm off-white
- **Alt Background (#F0EDE6)**: Alternating sections — warm cream
- **Card White (#FFFFFF)**: Cards, elevated content
- **Text Dark (#1a1a1a)**: Headings
- **Text Body (#4a4a4a)**: Paragraphs
- **Footer Dark (#1a1a1a)**: Footer background — dark anchor

## Layout Archetype
**GALLERY-FORWARD + NEIGHBORHOOD hybrid** (Layout Personality B+D)
- Hair salon where the work IS the product — showcase portfolio/transformations
- But also a deeply local neighborhood salon — address, hours, community language
- Gallery should be prominent and accessible
- Map and hours should be easy to find

## Section Order (Homepage)
Hero → Gallery Teaser (3-4 images linking to /gallery) → Primary CTA

## Full Site Section Order
- **Home**: Hero + gallery teaser + CTA
- **Team**: Lucy, Mary, Darlene — full profiles with philosophy and specialties
- **Gallery**: Portfolio grid showcasing hair work
- **Services**: Full service descriptions, senior discount, products used
- **Contact**: Map, hours, phone, email, booking CTA

## Layout Blueprint
- Hero: Full-bleed image (salon tools dark bg) with left-aligned text + CTA
- Gallery teaser: Asymmetric grid — one large image + 2-3 smaller
- Team: Individual cards with initials (no real profile photos), specialties, philosophy
- Services: Editorial list with dividers, not card grid
- Contact: Map prominent (half-page), hours + contact info

## Tone of Voice Guide
- **Greeting style**: "Hey there" not "Dear valued customer"
- **Selling style**: "Come see what Lucy can do" not "Schedule a consultation"
- **Community language**: "Alderwood's salon", "your neighborhood spot"
- **Confidence**: "Turning out some serious hair since the 1960s"
- **Never**: pushy, corporate, pretentious
