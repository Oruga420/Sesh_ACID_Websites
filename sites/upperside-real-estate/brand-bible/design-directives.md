# Design Directives — Upperside Real Estate Limited

## Identity Summary
Upperside Real Estate is a professional, established real estate brokerage in Vaughan with 70+ agents serving the Greater Toronto Area. The brand identity is clean, trustworthy, and authoritative — gold accents on white convey premium positioning while charcoal tones ground the design in professionalism. The site should feel like a top-tier brokerage that commands respect, not a template MLS portal.

## DO
- Use a light, clean background throughout — white with subtle warm gray alternating sections
- Feature gold (#bba545) as the primary accent for CTAs, highlights, and active states
- Use charcoal (#5c5b5a / #222222) for header, footer, and dark contrast sections
- Keep navigation minimal and uppercase with wide letter-spacing — authority look
- Feature the agent roster prominently — this is a team-driven brokerage with 70+ agents
- Use property listing photos from their Google Storage CDN
- Show the Vaughan/GTA service area prominently
- Use strong headlines that convey trust and expertise
- Include real business info: 905-597-9333, 5&6-7900 Bathurst St, Vaughan ON

## DON'T
- Don't use dark mode — the site is explicitly light-themed
- Don't use Playfair Display or any serif fonts — brand is modern sans-serif
- Don't use rounded pill buttons — keep corners at 6px for professional feel
- Don't fabricate stats ("500+ homes sold") — only use verified data
- Don't use playful or casual language — tone is professional and confident
- Don't overcrowd the homepage — hero + services teaser + agent preview + CTA
- Don't use stock photos of random people for agents — use initials on gold background as fallback

## Font Pairing
Heading: Montserrat — modern, authoritative, excellent weight range for professional real estate
Body: Open Sans — clean, highly readable, professional web standard
Nav: Montserrat at 600 weight, uppercase — authority and clarity

## Color Usage Rules
- Primary CTA: Gold (#bba545) button with white text on any background
- Section alternation: #ffffff → #f7f7f7 → #ffffff
- Header/footer: Charcoal (#222222) with white/gold text
- Never use gold text on white background — insufficient contrast. Gold is for buttons and accents only.
- Borders and dividers: #ececeb — subtle, not heavy

## Layout Archetype: SERVICE-FIRST
Rationale: Real estate brokerage where the agent team and service breadth are the conversion drivers. Trust and professionalism sell.

## Section Order (from archetype)
1. Hero — full-bleed property image with bold headline + search/CTA
2. Services — overview of what the brokerage offers (buying, selling, pre-construction)
3. Agents Preview — showcase of featured agents with link to full team page
4. About — brokerage story, credentials, GTA coverage
5. Testimonials — customer reviews and trust signals
6. Contact — map, address, phone, form

## Layout Blueprint
- Header: Sticky, charcoal (#222222) background, gold logo/text, uppercase nav, CTA button in header
- Hero: Full-bleed property image with dark overlay, centered white headline, gold CTA button
- Services: 3-column grid on light gray, icon + title + description, gold accent on hover
- Agents: Featured agent cards (photo/initials, name, role), "View All Agents" link to /agents page
- About: Split layout — text left, image right
- Footer: Dark charcoal, 4-column layout, gold accents, social icons, contact info

## Tone of Voice Guide
- Headlines should sound like: "Your Trusted Partner in GTA Real Estate"
- Body copy should sound like: "With 70+ experienced agents across Vaughan, Richmond Hill, and Toronto, we bring deep local expertise to every transaction."
- CTAs should sound like: "Find Your Dream Home" / "Connect With an Agent" / "Get a Free Evaluation"
