# Design Directives -- Esto es Colombia

## Identity Summary
Esto es Colombia is a warm, vibrant Colombian restaurant and bar on Dufferin Street in North York, Toronto. Founded by celebrated Cali-born chef Diana Patricia Arizala, the restaurant is a cultural hub for the Colombian and Latin community. The brand radiates authenticity, warmth, and Latin pride. The visual identity uses bright yellow (#FBF800) accents on a clean white base, with bold Montserrat uppercase buttons and warm Poppins/Open Sans typography. The overall feel is lively, welcoming, and unapologetically Colombian.

## DO
- Use a bright white (#FFFFFF) base background -- the real site is light-themed (Divi body default)
- Lead with a full-bleed atmosphere photo in the hero with a dark overlay for text contrast
- Use yellow (#FBF800) for all primary CTA buttons with black text and 0px border radius (sharp corners)
- Use Poppins for headings (weight 300-700 range, modern and clean)
- Use Open Sans for body text (weight 400, highly readable)
- Use Montserrat for buttons and nav items (uppercase, letter-spaced)
- Include real food photos from BlogTO feature throughout the site (bandeja paisa, cazuela de mariscos, empanadas)
- Show hours and location prominently -- this is a location-first restaurant
- Include bilingual touches (Spanish dish names, cultural pride)
- Feature customer reviews prominently -- the social proof is strong
- Use Colombian flag colors subtly (yellow, blue, red accents already present in the palette)
- Alternate sections between white and light gray (#F7F7F7) backgrounds

## DON'T
- Don't use dark backgrounds as the dominant theme -- the site is light/warm, not dark-moody
- Don't use Playfair Display or Inter -- these are generic defaults not used by this business
- Don't use rounded buttons -- their brand uses sharp 0px border-radius on CTAs
- Don't include a stats bar -- not present on their real site
- Don't use generic stock photos -- real BlogTO feature photos are available and verified
- Don't make the tone corporate or formal -- the brand is warm, personal, and culturally proud
- Don't bury the hours and address -- they must appear early (location-first archetype)
- Don't use English-only copy -- the brand naturally mixes Spanish and English

## Font Pairing
Heading: Poppins -- because it is used on the real site for selected headings with weight 300 elegance
Body: Open Sans -- because it is the declared body font in their CSS (font-family: Open Sans, Arial, sans-serif)
Nav/Buttons: Montserrat at weight 500-600 -- because their Divi theme uses it for all .et_pb_button elements at 20px uppercase

## Color Usage Rules
- Primary CTA: Yellow (#FBF800) button with black (#000000) text
- Links: Blue (#2EA3F2) with darker blue hover (#1A8CD8)
- Section alternation: White (#FFFFFF) -> Light gray (#F7F7F7) -> White
- Footer: Dark (#1A1A1A) background with white text
- Red (#FB0000): Use sparingly for urgency accents only (reserve, order now)
- Never use yellow text on white -- only as button/badge background
- Borders and dividers: Light gray (#E2E2E2)

## Layout Archetype: LOCATION-FIRST
Rationale: Colombian restaurant where the dining atmosphere, location on Dufferin Street, and community identity are the primary draw. Customers describe feeling like they are in Colombia. Hours, address, and the physical experience of the space are paramount.

## Section Order (from archetype)
1. Hero -- Full-bleed atmosphere photo with "The Best Colombian Restaurant & Bar in Toronto" headline, yellow CTA
2. Menu Highlights -- Featured dishes (bandeja paisa, cazuela de mariscos, empanadas) with real food photos and prices
3. About/Story -- Diana Arizala's story from Cali to Toronto, the mission to share authentic Colombian cuisine
4. Gallery -- Food and atmosphere photos from BlogTO feature
5. Reviews -- Customer testimonials from Google, Yelp, and BlogTO
6. Map + Hours -- Google Maps embed, full address (2848 Dufferin St), phone, hours
7. Contact/Reservations -- WhatsApp reservation link, phone, social media

## Layout Blueprint
- Header: Sticky, white background, logo left, nav right (Menu, Events, Our Story, Location, Reserve), hamburger on mobile
- Hero: Full-bleed restaurant atmosphere image with dark gradient overlay, centered headline, yellow CTA button
- Menu section: 3-column grid of dish cards with real food photos, dish name (Spanish), description, price
- About section: Split layout -- image left (Diana or restaurant interior), story text right
- Gallery: 2x3 masonry grid of food and atmosphere photos
- Reviews: Horizontal scroll/cards with star ratings, reviewer names, and verbatim quotes
- Map section: Full-width Google Map embed with business info overlay
- Footer: 3-column dark footer with logo, nav links, contact info, social icons

## Tone of Voice Guide
- Headlines should sound like: "The Best Colombian Restaurant & Bar in Toronto"
- Body copy should sound like: "Authentic recipes from Cali, Colombia -- prepared with love by Chef Diana Arizala since 2016"
- CTAs should sound like: "Reserve Your Table" / "Explore Our Menu" / "Order Online"
