# Design Directives — Condos and Castles Realty Inc.

## Identity Summary
An established, family-owned boutique real estate brokerage with 25+ years of downtown Toronto expertise and a heritage dating back to 1402. The brand conveys trust, professionalism, and deep local knowledge while maintaining an independent, personal touch that differentiates them from franchise brokerages. The current website (WordPress 2.6, no SSL, fixed 885px width) is dramatically outdated and does not reflect the prestige of the business.

## DO
- Use a light, warm, premium color palette — off-white backgrounds with navy and gold accents
- Lead with authority: heritage (since 1999), expertise, and one-stop-shop value proposition
- Use Cormorant Garamond for headings — elegant serif that matches the "neoclassical" brand identity
- Use DM Sans for body — clean, modern, highly readable sans-serif
- Include real team member photos from their website (all verified HTTP 200)
- Show testimonials prominently — their strongest social proof asset
- Keep navigation to 5 items max: Home, About, Services, Listings, Contact
- Use gold (#C9A84C) for premium accents and CTAs on navy backgrounds
- Include the family heritage story (since 1402) as a trust differentiator
- Highlight green/eco-friendly credentials subtly
- Use full-bleed hero with Toronto skyline/cityscape imagery
- Section alternation: warm off-white (#F8F6F1) to linen (#EDE8DD) to white cards

## DON'T
- Don't use dark mode — the site is light-warm-premium themed
- Don't use Playfair Display, Roboto, or Inter — use Cormorant Garamond + DM Sans
- Don't add a stats bar with fabricated numbers
- Don't use rounded/pill buttons — sharp 4px radius for professional authority
- Don't use playful colors or fonts — this is a serious, established brokerage
- Don't add a team grid unless using their real photos (which we have)
- Don't overcrowd the homepage — keep it editorial and spacious
- Don't use anchor links — every nav item gets its own page

## Font Pairing
Heading: Cormorant Garamond — elegant classical serif that echoes the "neoclassical" WordPress theme name and the 600-year family heritage. Distinguished and authoritative.
Body: DM Sans — clean geometric sans-serif that provides excellent readability and modern contrast against the serif headings. Professional without being cold.
Nav: DM Sans at 500 weight, uppercase, 0.08em letter-spacing — structured and authoritative navigation style.

## Color Usage Rules
- Primary CTA: Gold (#C9A84C) text on navy (#1B3A5C) background, or gold bg with dark text
- Hero: Full-bleed image with navy overlay (rgba(27,58,92,0.55))
- Section alternation: Off-white (#F8F6F1) → Linen (#EDE8DD) → White cards on either
- Header: Solid navy (#1B3A5C) — white logo/text on dark background
- Footer: Deep navy (#0D253F) with gold accents and white text
- Green accent (#2E7D32) only for eco-friendly badge, not as primary color
- Never use the old site's muddy tan (#C2BAA3) or olive green (#F5FDE6) — those are dated

## Layout Archetype: SERVICE-FIRST
Rationale: Real estate brokerage where trust, expertise, and service clarity drive conversions. The hero needs to establish authority and the CTA path to consultation/listing search.

## Section Order (from archetype)
1. Hero — Toronto cityscape with authority headline, dual CTAs (Schedule Consultation + Browse Listings)
2. Services — Buying, Selling, Renting with clear descriptions
3. About/Heritage — Founded 1999, family tradition since 1402, one-stop shop
4. Testimonials — Real client quotes (strongest asset)
5. Team Teaser — 3-4 key team members with real photos
6. Listings Teaser — Featured/recent properties
7. Contact — Map, address, phone, form

## Layout Blueprint
- Header: Sticky navy (#1B3A5C) header, logo text left, nav right, gold "Contact Us" CTA button
- Hero: Full-bleed cityscape image with dark navy overlay, left-aligned headline, dual CTAs
- Services: 3-column cards on linen background, icons optional, clear service names
- About: Split layout — image left, heritage story right
- Testimonials: Contrast section with navy bg, white text, gold accent stars
- Team: Cards with real photos on white bg
- Footer: 4-column dark navy, gold accents, all contact info

## Tone of Voice Guide
- Headlines should sound like: "Toronto's Boutique Brokerage Since 1999" — authoritative, specific, heritage-weighted
- Body copy should sound like: "With over 25 years of hands-on experience in downtown Toronto's most sought-after neighborhoods, our team guides you through every step of your real estate journey." — warm professional, benefit-focused
- CTAs should sound like: "Schedule a Consultation" / "Explore Our Listings" / "Get Your Property Valued" — professional invitations, not hard sells
