# Design Directives — Pupuseria Salvadorena & Authentic Mexican Foods

## Identity Summary
A warm, family-run Salvadoran and Mexican restaurant on Finch Ave W in North York. The brand is about authenticity, tradition, and affordability — handmade pupusas that taste like home. The visual identity should feel inviting and earthy, with deep maroon and warm gold accents on a clean light background. No corporate polish — this is a neighbourhood gem.

## DO
- Use warm light backgrounds with deep maroon (#990000) accent text and buttons
- Lead with food photos — pupusas, tamales, platters — every section should have real imagery
- Keep navigation simple: Home, Menu, About, Contact
- Show hours and address prominently — this is a location-first business
- Use the real logo from their CloudFront CDN
- Include real customer reviews — authenticity is the brand's strength
- Use warm serif for headings (Bitter) paired with friendly sans-serif body (Nunito)
- Hero must be the full-bleed banner image with dark overlay and a clear "Order Online" CTA

## DON'T
- Don't use dark backgrounds for the main page body — the site is light-themed
- Don't use Playfair Display or Inter — these are generic defaults
- Don't add a fake stats bar (years of experience, customer count, etc.)
- Don't use cold/corporate blue tones — this brand is warm earthy tones
- Don't use stock photos — use only verified real food photos from their site and review platforms
- Don't over-design — the charm is in simplicity and authenticity
- Don't use sharp angular modern design — rounded corners (8-12px) match the welcoming vibe

## Font Pairing
Heading: **Bitter** — warm traditional serif that conveys authenticity and heritage, perfect for a Salvadoran family restaurant
Body: **Nunito** — friendly rounded sans-serif that's approachable and easy to read
Nav: **Nunito** at weight 600, uppercase, slight letter-spacing — clean and structured

## Color Usage Rules
- Primary CTA: White text on maroon (#990000) background
- Section alternation: #FFFFFF → #FFF8F0 → #FFFFFF
- Footer: Dark (#2A2A2A) with white text and gold (#FFD700) heading accents
- Never use blue or purple — clashes with the warm Latin American identity
- Borders and dividers: light gray (#E5E5E5), not harsh black

## Layout Archetype: LOCATION-FIRST
Rationale: Salvadoran restaurant where foot traffic, neighbourhood identity, and hours/location drive visits. Menu and food photos lead the experience.

## Section Order (from archetype)
1. Hero — full-bleed banner with "Order Online" CTA and hours teaser
2. Featured Dishes — 3-column grid of food photos with descriptions
3. About/Story — brief paragraph about the restaurant's authenticity
4. Gallery — food and atmosphere photos
5. Reviews — real customer quotes
6. Map + Hours — embedded Google Maps and weekly schedule
7. Contact — phone, address, social links

## Layout Blueprint
- Header: Sticky, solid white bg, logo left, nav right, hamburger on mobile
- Hero: Full-bleed CloudFront banner image, dark gradient overlay, centered white text, maroon CTA button
- Content sections: Alternating white/warm-cream backgrounds, 1200px max-width, 80px section padding
- Footer: Dark (#2A2A2A), 3-column grid (about, hours, contact), gold heading accents

## Tone of Voice Guide
- Headlines should sound like: "Authentic Salvadoran Pupusas in North York"
- Body copy should sound like: "Handmade daily with traditional recipes — come taste the real flavours of El Salvador"
- CTAs should sound like: "Order Online" / "Call Us" / "Visit Us"
