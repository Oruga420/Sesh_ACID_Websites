# Design Directives — Niro Tharmakulasingam, RE/MAX Community Realty

## Identity Summary
Niro is a professional, results-driven RE/MAX broker in Scarborough serving the Greater Toronto Area. His brand communicates trust, expertise, and a family-oriented approach to real estate. The visual identity should feel clean, authoritative, and modern — a sharp contrast to his outdated Goodlayers WordPress site. Navy blue dominates as the trust color, with RE/MAX red as the action accent.

## DO
- Use a dark navy header (#003B6F) — the logo is WHITE text on transparent, so header MUST be dark
- Lead with a full-bleed hero image of Toronto/Scarborough with dark gradient overlay and left-aligned text
- Show Niro's professional RE/MAX headshot prominently on the About page
- Use real property staging photos from his website for listings/gallery sections
- Include testimonials with real client names — social proof is critical for real estate
- Make "Book a Free Consultation" the primary CTA — visible above fold and repeated
- Show areas served with real neighborhood images from his site
- Keep the overall theme LIGHT — white backgrounds, clean professional feel
- Use the navy/red RE/MAX-adjacent color scheme throughout
- Include phone number with tel: link for mobile tap-to-call
- Show office address with proper Scarborough location

## DON'T
- Don't use dark mode — the brand is light, clean, and professional
- Don't use Playfair Display, Roboto, or Inter — use Raleway (heading) + Open Sans (body)
- Don't add a stats bar with fabricated metrics
- Don't use rounded pill buttons — use 6px radius for professional look
- Don't use Unsplash stock photos — use the real property and portrait images from his site
- Don't use anchor links — every nav item gets its own page route
- Don't make the homepage a section dump — hero + service teaser + CTA only
- Don't use playful or casual typography — this is a professional service brand
- Don't use light-colored text on light backgrounds
- Don't add "As Seen In" or media logos — not present on his real site
- Don't use the map showing London UK — use proper Scarborough coordinates

## Font Pairing
- **Heading**: Raleway (600-800 weight) — geometric, modern, professional. Matches the clean authority of a real estate broker brand. NOT Droid Serif which was the Goodlayers default.
- **Body**: Open Sans (300-500 weight) — neutral, highly readable, universally professional. Pairs perfectly with Raleway.
- **Nav**: Raleway at 600 weight, uppercase, 0.05em letter-spacing — clean professional navigation

## Color Usage Rules
- Primary CTA: RE/MAX red (#C8102E) on white background — high visibility call to action
- Header: Navy (#003B6F) background, white text and logo — mandatory for logo contrast
- Section alternation: White (#FFFFFF) → Light gray (#F5F7FA) → White
- Hero: Dark navy overlay on property/skyline image, white text
- Footer: Very dark navy (#0A1628) — visual anchor
- Links: Bright blue (#1A6BB5) with navy hover
- Never use red for body text — reserve for CTAs and badges only
- Never use dark backgrounds on content sections — only hero and footer are dark

## Layout Archetype: SERVICE-FIRST
Rationale: Real estate is trust-driven. Niro's brand leads with expertise, client results, and professional services rather than visual portfolio. Services and testimonials should appear before any gallery content.

## Section Order (from archetype)
1. **Hero** — Full-bleed Toronto/property image, dark overlay, "Your Trusted Scarborough Real Estate Partner" headline, Book Consultation CTA
2. **Services Teaser** — 3-4 key services with icons, linking to /services
3. **Trust Signal** — Brief about text + testimonial quote, linking to /about
4. **Featured Listings Teaser** — 2-3 property cards linking to /listings
5. **CTA Section** — Full-width navy CTA bar with consultation booking

## Layout Blueprint
- **Header**: Sticky, navy (#003B6F) background, white logo left, nav right (5 items max), "Book Consultation" CTA button in red
- **Hero**: Full-bleed image (Toronto skyline or property), dark gradient overlay, left-aligned H1 + subtitle + CTA button
- **Content sections**: Max-width 1200px, alternating white/light-gray backgrounds, 96px vertical padding
- **Footer**: 4-column dark navy, contact info + social icons + areas served + RE/MAX branding

## Tone of Voice Guide
- Headlines should sound like: "Your Trusted Partner in Scarborough Real Estate"
- Body copy should sound like: "With 9 years of dedicated service across the GTA, Niro brings expert negotiation skills and a family-first approach to every transaction."
- CTAs should sound like: "Book Your Free Consultation" / "Get a Home Evaluation" / "Explore Our Services"
