# Design Directives — Any Income Any Credit Mortgages

## Identity Summary
A Brampton-based mortgage brokerage specializing in alternative lending — getting people approved when banks say no. The brand should convey **authority, trust, and empowerment**. The audience is people in difficult financial situations who need to feel that help is real and professional. The design must balance financial gravitas with warmth and approachability.

## DO
- Use deep navy (#1B4D6E) as the primary authority color — it says "financial institution" without being cold
- Use green (#2E8B57) for approval/success states — reinforces the "we get you approved" message
- Use gold (#D4920A) sparingly for premium highlights, star ratings, key stats
- Lead with empowering headlines that focus on possibility, not limitation
- Show real homes — Brampton-style suburban residential properties
- Keep the hero left-aligned with a strong headline and clear CTA
- Use DM Serif Display for headings — authoritative serif that conveys trust
- Use Source Sans 3 for body — clean, modern, highly readable
- Include service cards that clearly explain each mortgage type
- Feature the team with initials on navy backgrounds (no public photos available)
- Make the phone number prominent and clickable everywhere
- Use light backgrounds throughout (light-clean theme) with dark hero as contrast
- Include a FAQ section — mortgage clients have many questions

## DON'T
- Don't use dark backgrounds for body sections — this is a light-clean theme
- Don't use playful fonts or rounded pill buttons — this is serious financial services
- Don't use stock photos of random people as team photos — use initials on brand color
- Don't include a stats bar with fake metrics
- Don't use generic "Welcome" or "We are the best" headlines
- Don't use anchor links — every nav item gets its own page
- Don't use Playfair Display, Roboto, or Inter
- Don't make it feel like a bank — it should be warmer and more approachable than Big 5

## Font Pairing
Heading: DM Serif Display — authoritative serif that conveys financial trust and heritage without being stuffy
Body: Source Sans 3 — Adobe's professional sans-serif, highly readable, modern and approachable
Nav: Source Sans 3 at weight 600 — clean, professional, no uppercase transform needed

## Color Usage Rules
- Primary CTA: #1B4D6E on white/light backgrounds, white text
- Section alternation: #FAFBFC (main) → #F0F4F8 (alt) → #FFFFFF (cards)
- Hero: Dark overlay (#0D2137 at 75-85% opacity) over home imagery
- Footer: #0D2137 — dark anchor matching hero
- Accent gold (#D4920A): Only for highlights, badges, stars — never for large areas
- Green (#2E8B57): Approval badges, success checkmarks, secondary CTAs
- Never use green for headings or large text blocks

## Layout Archetype: SERVICE-FIRST
Rationale: Mortgage brokerage where clarity of service offerings and trust in expertise drives conversions. Clients need to quickly understand what solutions are available for their specific situation.

## Section Order (from archetype)
1. Hero — Empowering headline + CTA + background home image with dark overlay
2. Services Overview — Card grid of mortgage types (bad credit, self-employed, new immigrant, etc.)
3. Why Choose Us — Trust signals, years of experience, lender network
4. Testimonials — Client reviews with emphasis on approval success stories
5. Team — Adarsh and Harjeet with professional presentation
6. FAQ — Common mortgage questions
7. Contact — Phone, email, address, map embed

## Layout Blueprint
- Header: Sticky, white background, logo text left, nav right, "Get Pre-Approved" CTA button
- Hero: Full-bleed home image with dark gradient overlay, left-aligned text, strong CTA
- Services: 2x3 or 3x2 card grid on light-alt background, each card with icon + title + description
- Trust Section: White background, large centered heading, 3-column feature blocks
- Testimonials: Dark navy background for contrast inversion, white text, client quotes
- Team: Light background, two-column layout with initials avatars
- FAQ: Accordion-style on alternating background
- Footer: Dark navy, 4-column layout with contact, services, quick links, legal

## Tone of Voice Guide
- Headlines should sound like: "Your Credit Score Doesn't Define Your Future" / "Every Situation Has a Solution"
- Body copy should sound like: "We specialize in finding mortgage solutions when traditional banks say no. Whether you're self-employed, new to Canada, or rebuilding credit — we'll find a path to homeownership."
- CTAs should sound like: "Get Pre-Approved Today" / "Book a Free Consultation" / "Find Your Solution"
