# Design Directives — Hanh Beauty Salon & Spa

## Brand Personality Answers

1. **Dominant emotion**: Professional calm and wellness — a comprehensive spa experience
2. **Product type**: TRUST/EXPERTISE is the product — medispa, laser, RMT, multiple locations = authority
3. **Establishment**: Established business, 4 locations — heritage weight, authority feel
4. **Customer feeling**: Professional, well-cared-for, comprehensive — "they have everything"
5. **What would NEVER fit**: Playful/cutesy, dark/gritty, minimalist indie — this is a serious multi-location business

## Layout Personality: AUTHORITY + NEIGHBORHOOD (C+D Hybrid)

Multiple locations, medispa credentials, comprehensive services = authority brand. But rooted in Brampton, serving local community = neighborhood warmth.

## DO

- Use sage green (#6B8E7B) as the dominant brand color — nature, wellness, calm
- Feature the breadth of services (spa + medispa + hair + laser) prominently
- Show multiple locations as a strength — "4 locations across Brampton"
- Use editorial list layout for services — not equal card grids
- Use warm gold accents for premium/medispa service tiers
- Build trust with the variety of services available
- Keep heading serif (DM Serif Display) for authority, body sans (DM Sans) for readability
- Sharp corners on buttons (4px) — professional, not playful
- Vary section padding: hero breathes, services compact, CTA bold
- Full-bleed wellness gradient section for testimonials

## DON'T

- Don't use dark mode — this is a bright, clean wellness space
- Don't use Playfair Display or Roboto — use DM Serif Display + DM Sans
- Don't add a stats bar with fabricated numbers
- Don't use rounded pill buttons — sharp corners for authority
- Don't use stock photos — CSS gradients with brand colors for fallback
- Don't anchor-link — dedicated routes for each page
- Don't dump all sections on homepage

## Font Pairing Rationale

- **DM Serif Display** (headings): Elegant editorial serif that conveys authority. Single weight (400) is intentional — the letterforms do the work.
- **DM Sans** (body): Clean geometric sans from the same family — perfect pair, shared design DNA.

## Layout Blueprint

### Homepage
- Split hero: headline LEFT, subtle green gradient RIGHT
- 3 featured service categories (Spa, Medispa, Hair) linking to /services
- Location snippet with hours
- Single testimonial quote

### Services Page
- Tabbed or section-grouped by category
- Editorial line items with pricing
- Medispa section highlighted as premium

### About Page
- Story of the spa, multiple locations, years of experience
- Map of all locations
- Team/values section

### Gallery Page
- Dark bg, wellness-toned gradients
- Categories by service type

### Contact Page
- Both locations with maps
- Hours for each
- Booking CTA prominent
