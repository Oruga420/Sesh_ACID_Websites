# Design Directives — Executive Real Estate Services Ltd.

## Identity Summary
Executive Real Estate Services is a 15+ year Brampton-based brokerage led by Harmahinder Gaind, Broker of Record. The brand projects professional authority with genuine warmth — "exceptional services from exceptional people." The visual identity centers on corporate blue and red (matching their building-icon logo), clean white backgrounds, and trustworthy typography. The feel should be premium but approachable — a firm you'd trust with the biggest purchase of your life.

## DO
- Use clean white backgrounds with professional blue (#1a4b8c) as the dominant accent
- Feature Harmahinder Gaind prominently — he IS the brand, with 15+ years and 4.9 stars
- Lead with trust signals: years of experience, review count, core values
- Use the real logo (blue building with red accent) in the header
- Keep the tone warm but authoritative — "trusted advisor" not "corporate machine"
- Show the team — this is a people business
- Use the red accent (#d30023) sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Include real Google review quotes as social proof
- Make phone number and WhatsApp clickable — these are action triggers for real estate
- Use uppercase nav with letter-spacing for the corporate/professional feel
- Include service area cities prominently

## DON'T
- Don't use dark backgrounds as the dominant theme — this is a light, clean, professional site
- Don't use Playfair Display, Roboto, or Inter — use Montserrat + Open Sans
- Don't make it feel like a property portal (that's what they already have) — make it feel like a premium brokerage firm
- Don't use rounded pill buttons — keep them subtle rounded (6px) for professionalism
- Don't use playful or casual fonts — this is serious money
- Don't fabricate stats — use the real 4.9 stars / 107 reviews / 15+ years / 6+ agents
- Don't add a stats bar with made-up numbers
- Don't use stock photos — we have Harmahinder's real headshot
- Don't make the hero text-centered — left-aligned split layout with Harmahinder's photo

## Font Pairing
Heading: Montserrat (700-800) — geometric sans-serif that reads as modern, corporate, and trustworthy. Matches the clean lines of the building-icon logo.
Body: Open Sans (400-500) — highly readable, warm sans-serif that pairs well with Montserrat without competing. Professional but not cold.
Nav: Montserrat at 600 — uppercase with 0.05em tracking for the corporate nav bar feel.

## Color Usage Rules
- Primary CTA: white (#fff) text on blue (#1a4b8c) background
- Secondary CTA: blue text/border on transparent, fills blue on hover
- Section alternation: white (#fff) → cool gray (#f5f7fa) → white
- Hero: dark blue gradient overlay on lifestyle/property image
- Footer: dark navy (#1a2332) — grounds the page
- Red (#d30023) accent: ONLY for highlight moments — star ratings, urgent CTAs, the logo building accent
- Never use red as a primary section background — it overwhelms the professional blue
- Text on light bg: near-black (#1a2332) for headings, gray (#4a5568) for body

## Layout Archetype: SERVICE-FIRST
Rationale: Real estate brokerage where clarity of service offerings and trust/expertise drive conversions. Clients choose based on agent credibility, track record, and service range.

## Section Order (from archetype)
1. Hero — Split layout: compelling headline LEFT ("Your Home Journey Starts Here"), Harmahinder's photo RIGHT
2. Services overview — 6 core services in editorial grid (not card dump)
3. About / Story — Harmahinder's 15+ years, values, what sets them apart
4. Testimonials — Real Google reviews with star ratings
5. Listings teaser — 3-4 featured properties linking to /listings
6. Contact CTA — Full-width CTA with phone, WhatsApp, email

## Layout Blueprint
- Header: White bg, sticky, logo left, nav right, "Contact Us" CTA button in nav
- Hero: Split layout — headline + CTA left, Harmahinder cutout photo right, subtle blue gradient bg
- Services: 2x3 grid with icon + title + brief description, alternating section bg
- About: Two-column — text left (story, values, experience), image right
- Testimonials: Quote cards on cool gray bg with star ratings
- Contact: Map + form + all contact details on one page
- Footer: Dark navy, 4 columns: about, services, contact, social

## Tone of Voice Guide
- Headlines should sound like: "Exceptional Services From Exceptional People"
- Body copy should sound like: "With over 15 years of experience in the GTA market, our team brings transparency, dedication, and results to every transaction."
- CTAs should sound like: "Find Your Dream Home", "Get Your Free Evaluation", "Connect With Us Today"
