# Dania Latina Dominican Hair Salon — Design Directives

## Identity Summary
Dania Latina is a vibrant Dominican hair salon in Burlington, ON owned by Dania Smiley. No website exists — only Vagaro/Fresha booking pages and a very active Instagram. 345 posts, 940 followers, 4.9-star reviews. Specializes in Afro and curly hair — Dominican blowouts, extensions, keratin, coloring. The vibe is joyful, welcoming, and empowering — "they treat you like a princess" and "constantly singing and smiling." Bilingual English/Spanish service.

## Layout Personality: B (GALLERY-FORWARD) + D (NEIGHBORHOOD) Hybrid
- Transformation-focused: before/after and blowout videos are the product
- Neighborhood warmth: address, hours, walk-in friendly
- Joyful energy: warm colors, rounded shapes, movement

## DO
- Use vibrant coral-pink (#E85D75) as primary accent — it reflects Dominican warmth and femininity
- Use deep plum (#2D1B2E) for headings and contrast sections
- Use amber-orange (#F4A259) for secondary highlights and pricing
- Use Libre Baskerville for elegant headings
- Use Nunito Sans for warm, friendly body text
- Use rounded buttons (50px radius) — matches the joyful, welcoming personality
- Use rounded card corners (16px) for warmth
- Include bilingual English/Spanish section labels
- Reference Dominican hair techniques naturally — blowouts, keratin, extensions
- Keep backgrounds warm (creamy white #FFFAF5, soft peach #FFF0E6)
- Feature the "princess treatment" customer experience in copy
- Make booking CTA prominent — they currently have NO website
- Use gradient CTA buttons (coral to amber) for energy
- Include Vagaro booking link prominently

## DON'T
- Don't use cold whites, grays, or blues — this is a WARM, VIBRANT brand
- Don't use dark mode — site is light-themed with joyful energy
- Don't use Playfair Display, Roboto, or Inter
- Don't add stats bars — no fabricated metrics
- Don't use stock photos — CSS gradient fallbacks with brand colors
- Don't use sharp, corporate button styles — rounded = warm
- Don't be restrained/minimal — this brand has ENERGY
- Don't use anchor links — dedicated routes
- Don't dump all sections on homepage

## Font Pairing Rationale
- **Libre Baskerville** (heading): Warm serif with personality. Dominican cultural pride and elegance. Not Playfair.
- **Nunito Sans** (body): Rounded geometric sans-serif. Friendly, approachable, matches the "singing and smiling" atmosphere.

## Color Usage Rules
- **Coral-pink (#E85D75)**: Primary CTAs, accent borders, highlighted text. Energy and femininity.
- **Deep plum (#2D1B2E)**: Headings, dark sections, footer. Elegant contrast.
- **Amber (#F4A259)**: Pricing, secondary CTAs, hover states. Tropical warmth.
- **Soft peach (#FDE8D0)**: Card backgrounds, subtle sections. Gentle warmth.
- **Creamy white (#FFFAF5)**: Main background. Never cold white.

## Tone of Voice Guide
- **Joyful and empowering**: "Your hair deserves the royal treatment"
- **Culturally proud**: Natural references to Dominican hair techniques
- **Bilingual**: Spanish labels add authenticity — "Nuestros Servicios / Our Services"
- **Personal**: Name Dania and Natalia — customers know them
- **Warm**: "Like family" — not corporate
