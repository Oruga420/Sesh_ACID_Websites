# Design Directives — MorCan Financial Inc.

## Identity Summary
MorCan Financial is a Woodbridge-based mortgage brokerage founded in 1997 by Joe Taibi, a former Seneca College professor and TD Bank loans manager. The brand radiates trust, family values, and deep industry expertise. The logo features blue text with a distinctive red "C" — a subtle nod to Canada embedded in the brand name. The redesign should feel like a premium financial institution that still has a personal, family-run warmth — not a cold corporate bank, and not a flashy fintech startup.

## DO
- Use a clean, light background throughout — the brand is professional and transparent
- Lead with authority: headline should communicate 28+ years of expertise and 50+ lender access
- Show Joe Taibi's credentials prominently — FSRA licensed, former TD Bank, Seneca professor
- Use the blue (#1B4F8A) and dark navy (#0F2440) as primary identity colors
- Keep the red accent (#C8292E) extremely limited — logo "C" accent only, never for backgrounds
- Use DM Serif Display for headings — authoritative serif with modern personality
- Use DM Sans for body — clean, readable, pairs beautifully with DM Serif Display
- Organize services clearly — they have 14+ services, group them logically
- Feature real testimonials from agents and clients — they are powerful trust signals
- Include "Since 1997" prominently — 28+ years is a massive trust signal
- Make "Get Pre-Approved" and "Apply Now" the primary CTAs
- Include FSRA license number — regulatory compliance builds trust
- Use subtle card hover effects — lift with shadow increase

## DON'T
- Don't use dark theme — this is a light, clean, trustworthy financial brand
- Don't use Playfair Display, Roboto, or Inter — use DM Serif Display + DM Sans
- Don't add a stats bar with fabricated metrics — use real testimonials instead
- Don't use rounded pill buttons — keep them at 6px radius for professional feel
- Don't use playful fonts or colors — this is serious money, not a startup
- Don't make the hero too flashy — clean authority over visual noise
- Don't use the red accent color for large areas — it's only for small highlights
- Don't add team grid with stock photos — Joe is the face of the business
- Don't use heavy gradients — keep the look clean and understated
- Don't bury the phone number — many mortgage clients prefer calling

## Font Pairing
Heading: DM Serif Display — elegant serif with enough personality to avoid looking generic, but authoritative enough for financial services. Not as overused as Playfair Display.
Body: DM Sans — geometric sans-serif from the same DM family, creating visual harmony. Clean and highly readable at all sizes.
Nav: DM Sans at weight 500 — professional, no text-transform needed for a financial services brand.

## Color Usage Rules
- Primary CTA: white text on #1B4F8A blue
- Secondary CTA: #1B4F8A text with #1B4F8A border on transparent background
- Section alternation: #FFFFFF -> #F5F7FA -> #FFFFFF -> #0F2440 (dark for testimonials)
- Hero: dark navy overlay on property image, white text
- Footer: #0F2440 dark navy with white/muted text
- Never use red (#C8292E) for backgrounds or large text blocks — logo accent only
- Cards: white with #E5E7EB border, subtle shadow

## Layout Archetype: SERVICE-FIRST
Rationale: MorCan Financial is a trust-driven mortgage brokerage where clear service presentation and Joe Taibi's 28+ years of credibility are what convert visitors. The work isn't visual (like a tattoo studio) — it's about expertise, access to 50+ lenders, and a family atmosphere that makes clients feel secure about one of the biggest financial decisions of their lives.

## Section Order (from archetype)
1. Hero — authority headline + CTA + property image background
2. Services Overview — grouped mortgage services with icons/descriptions
3. Why Choose MorCan — trust signals: 50+ lenders, since 1997, FSRA licensed
4. Testimonials — real quotes from agents and clients (dark section for contrast)
5. About Joe Taibi — personal story, credentials, photo
6. Resources/Calculator teaser — link to calculator/resources page
7. Contact CTA — phone, email, address, map

## Layout Blueprint
- Header: White background, sticky with bottom border on scroll, logo left, nav right, "Apply Now" CTA button in nav
- Hero: Full-width with dark navy overlay on property photo, left-aligned text, headline + subheadline + dual CTAs
- Services: 3-column card grid grouped by category (residential, commercial, specialty), icons, clean descriptions
- Why Choose Us: 3 feature blocks with authority messaging, not a stats bar
- Testimonials: Dark navy (#0F2440) background section, white text, featured quotes with attribution
- About: Split layout — Joe's credentials left, trust messaging right
- Contact: Clean layout with phone, email, address, embedded map
- Footer: Dark navy, 4 columns (contact, services, resources, social)

## Tone of Voice Guide
- Headlines should sound like: "Your Mortgage. Your Terms. Since 1997."
- Body copy should sound like: "With access to over 50 lending institutions, we shop the market so you don't have to. Our job is to find you the right mortgage — not sell you the bank's."
- CTAs should sound like: "Get Pre-Approved Today" / "Talk to Joe" / "Start Your Application"
